Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, located in Sri Lanka's Galle District, is a renowned center dedicated to the conservation and protection of sea turtles. With its commitment to turtle conservation, the hatchery has become a popular destination for both locals and tourists who wish to learn about these fascinating creatures and contribute to their survival.

Visiting Hours

  • Open daily from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm.
  • Entrance fees are LKR 500.
  • Visit between April – July for the nesting season.
