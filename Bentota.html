Bentota Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery in Sri Lanka is a significant conservation center dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles. It serves as a sanctuary for various turtle species, providing a safe environment for nesting and hatching. Through educational tours and hands-on experiences, visitors learn about sea turtle conservation and contribute to the cause through donations and volunteering. The hatchery plays a crucial role in raising awareness and promoting responsible tourism to safeguard these remarkable creatures and their fragile ecosystems.

Visiting Hours

  • Open daily till 5:00 pm.
  • Entrance fees are LKR 500.
  • Visit between April – July for the nesting season.
